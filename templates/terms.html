Terms and Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before renting a house at Joy Apartments.

1. Deposit Amount

The deposit amount for renting a house is equal to one month's rent. This deposit is refundable under the conditions outlined below.

2. Rent Payment

Rent must be paid on or before the agreed date. Failure to pay rent on time will result in a penalty of 200 KES per day until the payment is made.

3. Contacting Authority

Before making any payment, you must first contact the management or authority to confirm availability and payment details.

4. Deposit Refund

The deposit will be refunded in full if you notify the management at least one month before relocating. Failure to provide this notice may result in partial or no refund of the deposit.

5. Electricity

Electricity is provided through prepaid tokens. Tenants are responsible for purchasing their own tokens to ensure uninterrupted power supply.

6. Water Bills

Tenants are responsible for paying their monthly water bills. The bills are calculated based on usage and must be paid directly to the water provider.

7. Garbage Disposal

A garbage disposal fee is charged monthly to cover waste collection and disposal services. This fee is mandatory for all tenants.

8. General Rules

  • No illegal activities are allowed on the premises.
  • Tenants are responsible for maintaining cleanliness and order.
  • Any damages to the property will be deducted from the deposit.
