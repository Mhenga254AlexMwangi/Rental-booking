Our Rental Agreement

Please read these terms carefully before renting with us. These conditions ensure a pleasant experience for all our tenants.

1 Deposit Policy

A security deposit equivalent to one month's rent is required upon signing the lease. This deposit is fully refundable provided:

  • No damages beyond normal wear and tear
  • All rent payments are current
  • Proper notice is given before vacating

2 Rent Payments

Rent is due on the 1st of each month. We offer a 5-day grace period, after which a late fee of 200 KES will be applied.

Pro Tip

Set up automatic payments to avoid late fees and ensure peace of mind.

3 Moving Out

To receive your full deposit refund, please provide written notice at least 30 days before your intended move-out date. This allows us time to process your request and find new tenants.

4 Utilities

Electricity

Our apartments use prepaid electricity tokens for convenience and fair usage. Tokens can be purchased at the management office.

Water

Water bills are calculated monthly based on meter readings and must be paid directly to the water provider by the 15th of each month.

5 Additional Fees

To maintain our high standards, we charge a monthly service fee that covers:

  • Garbage collection and disposal

6 Community Rules

We've established these guidelines to ensure all residents enjoy a peaceful living environment:

  • Quiet hours: 10PM - 7AM
  • Designated parking spots only
  • No drugs in common areas and if caught you are expected to vacate the same day without deposit

Have Questions?

Our management team is happy to clarify any of these terms. Contact us for personalized assistance.
